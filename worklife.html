My Work Life Journey

Despite the fact that my professional life began after graduating from college, my journey began much earlier. My journey started during my good old school days. That Chapter-16 Understanding HTML of computer science in 8th Standard in the school attracted me towards the World Wide Web. I still remember that index.html highlighting my name, Yanik Kumar, inside a <marquee> bright yellow background made me feel like I painted my name on the web. With the early access to the internet, I used to surf the web with curiosity, looking through different websites. The vibrant colors and glowing text attracted me.
Back in those days during 11th-12th standard, I used to learn to do magic for fun and came across a few online stores selling magic items.
